Our Promise

Returns & Replacements

Every box is made by hand, so opened boxes can't be resold — but you should never be stuck with a story that arrived hurt. If anything's wrong, we'll make it right, usually without asking you to mail a single thing back.

At a Glance

What We'll Do

Situation Our Promise Window

Missing a keepsake Missing item shipped, or full replacement 14 days of delivery

How to Start

Three Steps to Set It Right

01
Write to Us

Email within the window with your order number — and a quick photo if something arrived damaged. No forms, no return-merchandise codes.

02
We Make It Right

We'll re-seal a replacement or issue a refund within two business days. You'll never wait on hold or fight a chatbot to reach a person.

03
Keep the Keepsakes

For damaged or incomplete boxes, there's nothing to mail back. The wax, the print, the pressed botanical — those are yours to keep regardless.

The Details

A Few Specifics

Refund timing. Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method and typically appear within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank.

Limited editions. Hand-numbered limited editions are final sale because of their tiny run — but they're covered by the same damage and lost-in-transit guarantee as every other box.

Gifts. Either the gift-giver or the recipient can request a replacement for a damaged box — just reference the order number from the gift confirmation. More on the gift page.

Subscriptions. Returns and cancellations are separate things. You can cancel a subscription anytime from your account and keep every box already sent; see the FAQ for how billing works.

Something Not Right?

Tell us what happened and we'll fix it — quickly, kindly, and with a real person on the other end. Your story should arrive whole.
