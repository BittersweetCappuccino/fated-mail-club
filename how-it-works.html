The Ritual

How Fated Mail Works

No screens. No algorithms deciding what you'll love next. Just a slow, deliberate ritual — a story written by hand, sealed by hand, and sent to find you by moonlight. Here is exactly how an envelope makes its way to your door.

01

Choose Your Fate

Begin with a monthly subscription, or claim a single box from the archive while it lasts. Monthly readers receive each new story the moment it's sealed; annual readers save a little and never miss a season. Every tier carries a different depth of enchantment — but each one is a complete, self-contained story arc. There's no wrong way in.

02

The Story is Written

A rotating roster of romantasy authors writes an original novelette for each box — never before published, never to be reprinted once its season closes. We pair every story with a commissioned illustrator, so the world you fall into has a face. By the time it reaches you, dozens of hands have already loved it into being.

03

Sealed by Hand

In our studio in Portland, each package is assembled one at a time. The novelette is folded into a handcrafted envelope alongside its keepsakes — a pressed botanical, a wax-sealed secret, a letter from the author. Then it's stamped, ribboned, and closed with a drop of molten wax pressed beneath our seal. Nothing leaves the studio untouched.

04

It Finds You

One day it's simply there — waiting on your doorstep, your name inked across the front. Clear an afternoon. Brew something warm. Break the seal slowly. What happens next is just you, the paper, and a story that was made to be held. No notifications. No next episode. Only the one you're in.
