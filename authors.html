The Hands Behind the Wax

Our Authors

Every box is written by a different voice. We commission storytellers we adore — established and emerging — and give each one a single season to build a world from scratch. These are the writers whose words you've been holding.

EV
Eluned Vance
Court Fantasist

Eluned writes glittering, knife-edged fae politics where every kindness is a maneuver and every vow has teeth. She drafts longhand, by candlelight, and refuses to name her courts until the last page.

Wrote the boxThe Court of Eternal Frost
MT
Marisol Trench
Saltbound Romantic

A former marine biologist turned mythmaker, Marisol writes the sea as a character — tidal, jealous, and tender. Her drowned kingdoms feel less invented than remembered.

Wrote the boxWhere the Tides Remember
CR
Cassian Roe
Wayfinder of the Wilds

Cassian maps liminal places — the forest that only appears at midnight, the door between one breath and the next. His shapeshifters are always running toward something, never away.

Wrote the boxThe Wilds Between Worlds
PH
Perdita Hollow
Keeper of Thorns

Perdita grows gothic gardens out of grief and longing. Her cursed royals bloom in the dark, and her sentences have a way of pricking you exactly where you weren't guarding.

Wrote the boxA Crown of Thornlight
IV
Ignatia Vesper
Ember Scribe

Ignatia specializes in the slowest of slow burns — oaths sworn through gritted teeth, hands that take three hundred pages to finally touch. She writes warmth like it costs something. Because, in her worlds, it does.

Wrote the boxThe Cinderwake Vows
SQ
Soraya Quill
Astral Lyricist

Soraya writes in the register of myth and elegy — star-crossed in the most literal sense. A poet first, she lets her prose break into verse whenever a heart does. Her box sold out in a single afternoon.

Wrote the boxInk of the Fallen Stars
