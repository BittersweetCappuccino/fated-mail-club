Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked

Everything you might wonder before the first envelope lands — billing, shipping, what's tucked inside, and how gifting works. Still curious after this? Write to us.

Subscriptions & Billing
How does a subscription work?
Choose monthly or annual when you subscribe. Each month we seal a brand-new, original romantasy story and send it your way. Monthly readers are billed on the date they join and again on that date each month; annual readers pay once and save 10%.
Can I cancel anytime?
Always. Cancel before your next billing date and you won't be charged again — and you keep every box already sent. No cancellation fees, no maze of menus. There's a single button in your account, and we'll never make you talk us out of it.
When will I be charged?
On the day you join, then on that same calendar day each month (or once a year for annual plans). We email a gentle reminder a few days before every renewal.
Can I pause my subscription?
Yes — life gets full. You can pause for one, two, or three months from your account and pick the story back up whenever you're ready. Paused months are simply skipped, never charged.
Shipping & Delivery
When will my first box arrive?
Your first envelope is hand-assembled and posted within a week of subscribing. After that, boxes ship in the first week of each month. Full timing and carrier details live on our shipping page.
Where do you ship, and how much is it?
Shipping is free within the United States. We post internationally to most countries at cost, calculated at checkout. See the full list of regions and estimated transit times on the shipping page.
My box hasn't arrived — what now?
Mail magic occasionally misfires. If your envelope is more than a few days late or arrives damaged, contact us and we'll re-seal and re-send a replacement, no questions asked.
The Boxes
What's actually inside a box?
Every box holds an original illustrated novelette, a letter from its author, and a handful of themed keepsakes — a wax-sealed secret, a pressed botanical, a map or print. Contents vary by season; you can see exactly what came in past boxes in the archive.
Are stories ever reprinted?
No. Each story is printed once for its season and then sealed forever in the archive. The only exceptions are our rare, hand-numbered limited editions — and even those never come back once they sell out.
Who are the boxes for?
Grown-up lovers of romantasy. Our stories carry adult romantic themes — swoony rather than explicit — and are written for readers who want fantasy and a love story in equal measure. Meet the writers on our authors page.
Gifting & Returns
Can I send this as a gift?
Beautifully. Gift a single box or a 3-, 6-, or 12-month run, choose the arrival date, and add a note we'll hand-letter onto a card tucked inside the first envelope. Start one on the gift page.
What's your return policy?
Because each box is handmade and includes keepsakes, opened boxes aren't returnable — but if something arrives damaged or incomplete, we'll always make it right. The full policy lives on our returns page.

Still Wondering?

If your question slipped between the cracks, we'd genuinely love to answer it. A real person reads every message.
