Good to Know

The Fine Print

Tracking. Every shipment is fully tracked, domestic and international. If your tracking hasn't moved in several days, or your box arrives damaged, contact us and we'll re-seal and re-send a replacement at no charge.

Customs & duties. International orders may be subject to import duties or taxes set by your country. These aren't included in our shipping cost and are the recipient's responsibility — we declare every parcel accurately as a book and keepsakes.

Address changes. Moving? Update your shipping address from your account before the 25th of the month and your next box will follow you to your new door. PO boxes are perfectly welcome.

Gifts. Gifting a box? You can choose the arrival date and ship straight to the recipient — details on the gift page.
