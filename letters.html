Letters From the Sealed

Our readers write back. They send us photographs of their unboxing rituals, pressed petals from boxes past, and notes that make our whole studio go quiet. Here is a wall of the words they've sent us — unedited, and deeply kept.

I've been a subscriber since the very first box. Every month I clear an afternoon just to unwrap it slowly. It's the most romantic experience I have with fiction — and I've read thousands of books.

MV
Mirabel V.
3-year subscriber

The wax seal alone made me cry. Then I read the story and completely lost it. Whoever is picking these authors has impeccable taste — every single arc has hit me somewhere deep.

TC
Theo C.
The Tides box, Sep 2024

I gift this to my best friend every year for her birthday. She literally squeals. We have a ritual now where we read the same box and call each other to debrief. It's sacred at this point.

AL
Amara L.
Gift subscriber

I keep every envelope. They live in a wooden box on my shelf and I genuinely think they'll be the thing I grab if the house ever catches fire. No app has ever made me feel like that.

RB
Rowan B.
2-year subscriber

The Cinderwake Vows undid me. I read the last page on my fire escape at midnight and just sat there. Then I lit the little vow candle and read it again. Who does that to a person?

JN
Juniper N.
The Cinderwake box, Dec 2023

My partner is not a reader. Was not a reader. The first box arrived for me and now we trade it back and forth and argue about the characters over dinner. You've changed my marriage, a little.

DP
Delia P.
1-year subscriber

I've never written to a company before in my life. But the pressed botanical in the Wilds box was the exact flower from my grandmother's garden, and I needed someone to know it found its way to me.

SK
Soren K.
The Wilds box, Jun 2024

As someone who stares at screens for a living, having a story that lives entirely on paper feels like a small rebellion. I read it in the bath. No notifications. It's the best forty dollars I spend all month.

FH
Fern H.
Monthly subscriber

I missed the Fallen Stars box and I will never financially or emotionally recover. Please bring back archived runs. I am not above begging. The constellation print my friend got glows. I want one.

IM
Isolde M.
Newsletter reader
